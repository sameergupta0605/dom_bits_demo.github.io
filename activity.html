Interface

INTERFACE is a pan India management fest organized by the institute and the legacy event of the Department of Management, BITS Pilani - Pilani Campus. The fest is a 3-day extravaganza combining academic vigor and industry insights. This year the department is organizing the 45th edition of Interface themed “Inculcating Innovation” In order to become a global powerhouse, India needs innovation in the form of knowledge accumulation and better human capital that can contribute towards the promotion of economic growth. Indian innovation is characterized by "Jugaad" - a frugal, flexible, and inclusive approach to problem-solving and innovation (Jaideep Prabhu,2014). Though there is a lot of talk about innovation in India, there is a lack of an ecosystem that drives most of the innovations in the West. The need of the hour is enabling the youth of India who have the potential to generate original ideas to unleash their creativity and unshackle their innovative spirit to transform society. "Interface - Inculcating Innovation", aspires to provide a common arena to generate ideas, especially those based on data and technology, creating a network among academicians, practitioners, and students to brainstorm on the ideas in order to supercharge innovation.
