Programme Overview

MBA in Business Analytics is a two-year full-time residential program aimed at creating business analytics professionals employable by leading Indian and foreign firms. The business analytics area makes use of sophisticated quantitative and statistical analysis and predictive modeling towards building competitive strategies around data-driven insights that in turn generate impressive business results. The MBA in Business Analytics program integrates technology with statistical and quantitative modeling techniques to provide students with the foundation needed for data-driven decision making, as well as for graduate study in the field of Business Analytics. The focus of the program is on the application of analytics and Technology in all the functional areas of management, including but not limited to, marketing, finance, operations, human resources, entrepreneurship etc.

Course Structure

Management education at BITS Pilani is anchored in the fundamentals of general management with the latest theories and best practices in the business / corporate world. It builds a basic framework of qualitative, quantitative, analytical, strategic and problem solving skills. In addition, courses on programming, statistics, analytics and deep learning will prepare students to develop solutions and build AI products to solve complex business problems. The pedagogy is specially designed to create a healthy learning environment, a blend of theoretical concepts and practical nuances. An optimal mix of learning methods ensures a holistic personality development of the students as well.
