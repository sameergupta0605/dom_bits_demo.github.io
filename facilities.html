Classrooms

The campus is divided into different academic blocks for smooth delivery of lectures to a large number of students specializing in different fields of study. Most of the lectures for management courses are delivered in the New Academic Block (NAB).

Library

Library has room for up to 8,000 students, offers 738 open access study places and 246 open-access IT work stations. As well as the books and periodicals you expect, it is home to computing facilities for students.

Bloomberg Finance Lab

In association with the Department of Economics and Finance, the Department of Management had developed Bloomberg Finance Lab to Bloomberg Finance Lab for the use of students and researchers at BITS Pilani.

Databases

To facilitate data access for research and applied projects, students are provided access to the Capitaline and CMIE ProwessIQ databases. Taken together, both database provides fundamental and market data for more than 389,000 Indian companies (both listed and unlisted).
