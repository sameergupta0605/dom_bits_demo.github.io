Message from Head of Department

The Department of Management, Pilani Campus currently offers a two-year MBA in Business Analytics program and a Ph.D. program in Management. Management education at BITS Pilani brings cutting-edge thinking and best learning practices to its flagship MBA in Business Analytics program through three semesters of rigorous classroom training coupled with provision for 6 months of exposure to the industry through a structured and evaluative internship (Practice School) making the program unique. The MBA in Business Analytics program provides students with the foundation needed for data-driven decision making, as well as for graduate study in the field of Business Analytics. The curriculum balances a depth of analytical training with a breadth of business knowledge. In the MBA in Business Analytics program, students will learn hardcore skills in machine learning, deep learning algorithms, optimization, etc., and apply these to several kinds of business problems. Our teaching methods include classroom lectures, workshops, case studies, individual and group projects, analytical modeling guest lecturers, etc. BITS Pilani management program enhances value through its connection to an enviable alumni network spread across the world at key positions in corporate and academia. I would like to cordially invite all the interested students, researchers, alumni, experts from industry and academia, and prospective employers to explore this website to know more about the several initiatives undertaken by the department and the excellent learning environment at BITS Pilani.
