Message from Director

The Department of Management at BITS Pilani established in 1971 endeavors to prepare impactful leaders of the future and aims at advancing management knowledge. With a robust curriculum having strong industrial component and collaboration, and through various innovative pedagogies, it delivers cutting-edge management education in a friendly learning environment. The focus is not only on producing managers, but also on developing innovative and result-oriented, yet socially responsible, individuals who will be eager to work for the greater good of the human kind. The Department is continuously evolving with the tireless efforts put forth by a vast alumni base, excellent faculty members and state of the art infrastructure, apart from many other important factors. I welcome all aspiring students, researchers, alumni, experts from industry and academia, and potential recruiters to explore your opportunities for growth by getting involved with the Department of Management. We are here to nurture the brightest of minds to sharpen their business skills and facilitate their holistic development. I am confident that our faculty members and teams of scholars in the Department of Management can also help organizations progress further through their expertise in different domains. Finally, I reaffirm my faith in knowledge being the supreme power. Let us cherish this power together for a brighter future.
